About the Program

This intensive program features Harvard Crimson editors—some of the best collegiate writers in the nation—providing exclusive insight and education for high school students about their college application process. The program includes engaging discussions and workshops run by the Crimson staff and a guest speaker dedicated to reviewing the Common Application’s personal statement and additional supplemental college essays, developing long-term strategies to excel in the college admissions process, and preparing for college interviews. Students will also participate in personalized, individual review sessions, during which each high school student will be paired up with a Harvard undergraduate and will receive a comprehensive, tailored review of their college application, essays, and high school career.

The College Insight Program is a unique experience that high achieving students can take advantage of to excel in college applications and gain exclusive understanding of Harvard’s admissions process.
