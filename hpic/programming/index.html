Conference Programming

Entrepreneurship Identify and practice the key characteristics that have cultivated and advanced entrepreneurs for success. We'll explore new technological advancements in the world of start-ups and address how to develop a project from vision to execution.
Powerful Communication Discover powerful ways that you can communicate -- through email, in person dialogue, or on the phone. You'll practice and enhance how you present yourself and your voice to make a powerful first impression and be an effective leader in your community.
Career Development Perfect the most important aspects of networking and tailoring the application process, from interviews to follow up emails. The critical skills necessary when applying for an internship are applicable beyond the work setting.
College Applications Prepare for and get a head start on the college application process with advice from current Harvard students. We are here to help provide guidance and answers through panel discussions, a Q&A session, and personal application feedback.
