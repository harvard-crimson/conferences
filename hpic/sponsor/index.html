Sponsor

Cisco Systems, a multinational technology conglomerate, is a Sponsor of the 2018 Harvard Pre-Collegiate Innovation Conference. Programming includes a Case Study that highlights the innovative work Cisco has accomplished as well as Pre-Conference reading materials (below) about the Digital Future, a topic that this firm is taking the lead on in this industry. Su Zhe, a Systems Engineer from the Cisco Beijing Office, will join students this summer at HPIC to lead a discussion and inspire them to think critically and creatively about the future of technology
